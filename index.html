Fantastic Animals

Fox

Foxes are mammalian and omnivorous animals belonging to the Canidae family. They are medium-sized vulpids, characterized by a long snout and a long, furry tail.

They also have as a particularity their oval pupils, similar to the vertical pupils of felids.

Of about 40 species recognized as foxes, only 12 belong to the genus Vulpes of the "true foxes", of which the red fox is the most common.

They also have as a particularity their oval pupils, similar to the vertical pupils of felids.
